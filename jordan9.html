Air Jordan 9

On October 6th, 1993, Michael Jordan shocked the sports world by announcing that he’d be retiring from the game of basketball.

The Jordan IX was Infamous for being the only “early” Air Jordan model Michael would never wear in the NBA, the Air Jordan IX would make appearances during the 1993-94 season anyway, and was even forever forged in monument outside the Chicago Bulls’ home arena. The sneaker still appeared in a Birmingham Barons baseball game as you can see in the image above. Jordan chose to play baseball during his retirement from basketball.
