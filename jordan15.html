Air Jordan 15

The Air Jordan 15 was the family’s first release after Michael Jordan’s (2nd) retirement from the NBA. The shoe never got the honor of being worn by him to score and win. Michael Jordan announced his (2nd) retirement in Jan 1999. Most observers thought the end of Jordan’s NBA career meant the end of Air Jordan line, but there were some who thought they could still go on with it.

The Air Jordan 15’s design was inspired by NASA’s X-15, an aircraft for space exploration. Actually, it’s a bit tricky here. There’s also a controversy that Nike got part of the idea from Prada, the Italian shoemaker, but they deny it. So, the source of inspiration behind the design is a bit unclear.But not unclear are the specifics of the Jordan 15. It had a hidden quick-lace system, seamless dynamic-fit sleeve, Kevlar mesh uppers, and Zoom Air sole.
