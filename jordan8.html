Air Jordan 8

ordan 8 holds a place in the Air Jordan hall of fame that no other model in the series does. Nike released it in the 1992-93 “Three Peat Championship” season, and Michael Jordan wore them to pulverize the Hawks, Knicks, Cavs, and Suns. Wearing these, Jordan reached his pinnacle before his first retirement.

Michael Jordan led the Bulls to get them the NBA title for the third time. For himself, Jordan got the scoring title for the seventh time (straight) and hit a milestone by scoring 20,000 points in his NBA career.

The Air Jordan 8 didn’t lack innovation either. Tinker Hatfield made sure that it had a well-crafted design that gave maximum performance boost to the player.The unique upper with a lockdown strap and a chromatic midsole gave it a catchy appearance, and you could be sure of being noticed in the court if you’re wearing them.They will let you bend and jump effortlessly, and the light weight makes it easy to spring back from your position.
