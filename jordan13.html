Air Jordan 13

The Air Jordan 13 was a product of years of yearning, of craving, of subliminal attraction, and of inspiration itself. And, when Tinker Hatfield was able to compile all of his creative brainstorming, the product would be as unique as it was effective.

Not many people know about Air Jordan 13. It’s one of those rare jewels that get eclipsed under something else, but what’s hidden is not always worthless. Many a times, it’s an immortal treasure that needs to be discovered. Air Jordan 13 is one such treasure.

The shoe also boasted a panther’s eye hologram on the upper, and the lower part of the shoe was meant to look like a paw. Jordan wore the black colorways on the road and the white ones while playing at home. In addition to its cat like design, the Jordan 13 is said to be one of the most comfortable shoes in the Jordan line. This can be attested to its Python midsole and its heel and forefoot Zoom Air.
